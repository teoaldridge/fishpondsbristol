Yoga with Nikki

Tuesdays 8:15pm-9:15pm
Wednesdays 5:45pm-6:45pm
Thursdays 9:30am-10:30am
Thursdays 6pm-7pm

This class will help give you overall flexibility, build your fitness, bring you more strength and balance and relax your mind. You will stretch out tight muscles to reduce aches and stiffness and allow your mind to switch off from everyday work and life stress. All are welcome including absolute beginners of any age and fitness levels.

£5.50 (no pre-booking).

Zumba with Steph

Saturdays 9:30am - 10:30am

Zumba® is a total body workout, combining all elements of fitness: cardio, muscle conditioning, balance and flexibility. Classes are designed for everybody, mixing low-intensity and high-intensity moves for an interval-style, calorie-burning dance fitness party!

Women's Reading Group

Every 3d Wednesday of each month 1:30pm - 3:00pm

The group was established over 15 years ago for women to read books by women writers. We keep broadly to this principle. We meet on the 3rd Wednesday of each month from 1.30-3.00. We read a wide range of books, suggested by the members, and obtained via the local library. We occasionally go to local book festivals e.g. Wells, Bath, Hawkesbury Upton.

t: 0790536609

Testimonials

"Beechwood Club is my favourite place to go for exercise classes. We hired the Beechwood Club hall for my son's fifth birthday and I was so impressed with the kitchen and amount of space we got for the price!"

Laura, local resident

"We are so lucky to have this hall in our area. I frequently attend classes there and in the past have organized a playgroup which was held in Beechwood Club. The hall is beautiful and the facilities are fantastic!"

Teodora, local resident and former organizer of the Bulgarian Playgroup in Bristol

"Every year the members of the Beechwood Club (FLAG), give our local Rainbow Unit, Brownie Pack, Guide Company help and support. We hold a Christmas Fair each year and they kindly donate the use of the hall for this - with no charge. Helping us to make the most of the funds we raise each year.
As members of Girlguiding we rely on subscriptions from parents and continued fundraising - this is our only income throughout the year. So maximising what we make at events is priceless.
Not only that but members of the committee also give up their time to help set up and clear up, and many of them of them support us on the day.
They have also been welcoming and supportive and are keeping a great asset to Fishponds open - well maintained and accessible."

A member of a local troup of Guides and Brownies
