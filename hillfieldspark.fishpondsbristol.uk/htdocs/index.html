BMX Club

The Hillfields BMX club is run by local volunteers and parents in collaboration with Access Sport.
The club offers the following BMX sessions, led by qualified BMX coaches:

• Beginners (boys and girls) - Saturdays 10:15am- 11:15am, £2 per session.
• Intermediates (boys and girls) - Saturdays 11:15am-12:15am, £2 per session.
• Girls-only - Thursdays 5:00pm-5:45pm, £2 per session.
• Women-only - Thursdays 6:00pm-7:00pm, £3 per session.
• Free sessions during the school holidays – Tuesday mornings.

BMX bikes and helmets are provided by the club for the duration of the sessions.

For more information and to book a space, please visit our Facebook page or contact:

Hillfieldss Community Garden

A new community garden in Hillfields Park!


We are a group of local residents and volunteers who have come together to develop a growing project which will bring the community together benefiting all ages.

We started working on this project during the COVID lock down of 2020 and since then, the council have agreed to us using an area in Hillfields Park to create a community garden.
So far, we have built and planted two raised beds, and have plans to build more of them, put in a wildflower area and an orchard.
We are currently working on the design of the community garden, after which we will be looking for funding for the next stage of its development.

The community garden currently hosts the following regular free of charge social groups:

• Gardening for Wellbeing
• Wild Women
• Mini Explorers
• Volunteer Gardening Group

We organized a Community Garden Picnic in June 2021, which was a great success! A lot of local residents joined the picnic and many said that they would love to attend more social gatherings in Hillfields Park. We are planning to organise and use the Hillfields Community Garden & Hillfields Park space for similar events in the future.

Please visit our Hillfields Community Garden web page for more information.
